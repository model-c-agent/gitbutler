## Key Findings

### All 30 Tier 1 orgs placed in the top 30
The correlation between tier and score is near-perfect. Tier 1 organizations — those with full agent rosters and detailed proposals (300-500 lines) — swept the top 28 positions. Only one Tier 2 org (grep_the_stacks, #150) cracked the top 30, at position 29.

### The top 5 span 4 domains and 4 philosophies
No single domain or philosophy dominates. The winners come from Fashion Design (2), Maritime Logistics, Insurance Actuarial, and Library Science. The philosophies span Academic Research Lab (2), Artist Commune, Anarchist Collective, and Startup Hustle.

### Memory creativity was the differentiator
Among the top 10, all scored 80+ on Technical Soundness. The separation came from Memory/Identity Creativity:
- **Woven memory** (083) — warp/weft interlacement with weave patterns
- **Narrative memory** (084) — chapters, motifs, dramatic tension
- **Manifest memory** (001) — CRDT-based distributed manifests with tidal cycles
- **Actuarial-table memory** (093) — fitted survival functions per entry
- **Card-catalog memory** (145) — five simultaneous classification systems
